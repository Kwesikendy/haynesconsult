Founder & Lead Strategist

Dr. Senyah

Clinical Excellence Meets Digital Innovation

Dental Surgeon • Human Biologist • AI Consultant & Automation Specialist

An award-winning Dental Surgeon and Digital Health Innovator, Dr. Atta Boateng Junior Senyah sits at the unique intersection of clinical practice, artificial intelligence, and digital strategy. Currently at Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital (Ghana's premier tertiary hospital), he holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery from KNUST, is pursuing a Doctorate of Business Administration in AI & Machine Learning from Walsh College, and is an internationally recognized Diplomate in Aesthetic Medicine from IBAMS.

Beyond the clinic, Dr. Senyah builds and scales robust digital infrastructures. As the Founder and Lead Strategist for Haynes Consult, he replaces systemic inefficiencies with flawless automation. He has a proven track record of scaling enterprises—from driving 400 new patients and 100,000+ GHS in revenue for a clinic in just 4 months, to leading global digital campaigns for the Ghana Dental Association reaching over 130,000 people.

A committed leader and visionary, he serves as a board member for the Ghana Dental Association's National Congress, the World Oral Health Day committee, and the Ghana Cleft Lip & Palate Association. He has been repeatedly honored for his strategic impact, winning the FDI Best Media Award completely natively for two consecutive years (2024, 2025), and the KNUST School of Dentistry Innovation Award.

Professional Achievements & Credentials

  • Dual-Disciplinary Mastery: Integrates clinical expertise (BDS, IBAMS Diplomate) with deep technical acumen (ongoing DBA in AI & Machine Learning).
  • AI & Systems Integration: Architect of comprehensive research databases, custom localized EHR models, and advanced automated workflow integrations.
  • National Representation: Executive leadership and media direction for the Ghana Dental Association and Ghana Cleft Lip & Palate Association.
  • Award-Winning Campaign Strategy: Two-time consecutive recipient of the FDI Best Media Award, alongside the GDA Most Versatile Student Award.
  • Educational Impact: Keynote speaker and guest lecturer on the practical applications of AI in modern clinical dentistry at KNUST School of Dentistry.
