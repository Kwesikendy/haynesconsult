Founder & Lead Strategist

Dr. Senyah

Clinical Excellence Meets Digital Innovation

Dental Surgeon • Human Biologist • AI Consultant & Automation Specialist

An award-winning Dental Surgeon and Digital Health Innovator, Dr. Atta Boateng Junior Senyah sits at the rare intersection of clinical practice, artificial intelligence, and high-impact digital strategy. Currently completing housemanship at Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital — Ghana's premier tertiary referral institution — he holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery from KNUST, a Doctorate of Business Administration in AI & Machine Learning from Walsh College (Michigan), and is an internationally recognized Diplomate in Aesthetic Medicine from IBAMS (New York).

Beyond the clinic, Dr. Senyah architects and deploys robust digital infrastructures that produce measurable results. As founder of Haynes Consult, he replaces systemic inefficiencies with precision automation — from building custom EHR systems and ICD-10 coding frameworks for surgical departments, to engineering nationwide dental databases and designing the official GDA website used by thousands of professionals across Ghana.

His campaigns have broken records at national scale. The WOHD March 2025 digital campaign he led generated 130,000+ Snapchat reach (95% engagement), grew LinkedIn impressions by 687.6%, drove 20,000 active website users across 7+ countries, and increased event attendance by 300%. A single Snapchat Face Lens filter he created racked up 158,499 views and 18,650 plays.

A committed leader, he serves on the board of the GDA's 33rd–35th Annual Congress, the World Oral Health Day Committee, and the Ghana Cleft Lip & Palate Association's Constitution and Steering Committees. He has won the FDI Best Media Award two consecutive years (2024 & 2025), the KNUST School of Dentistry Innovation Award, the Excellence & Creativity Award, and the GDA Most Versatile Student Award.
