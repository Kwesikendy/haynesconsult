Healthcare & Clinic Management

Multi-Chain Clinic EHR Integration

The Problem

A regional healthcare provider was bleeding revenue due to decentralized patient records across three clinics, leading to 40% patient drop-off in follow-up appointments and severe administrative burnout.

The Solution

We engineered a bespoke, cloud-based Electronic Health Record (EHR) system featuring automated SMS follow-ups, integrated online booking, and a centralized analytics dashboard for the administrative department.

AI Customer Support Funnel

The Problem

A rapidly scaling D2C retail brand was overwhelmed by thousands of repetitive customer inquiries regarding shipping status, causing their human support team response times to plummet to over 48 hours.

The Solution

We deployed an advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) chatbot integrated directly into their logistics API. It automatically resolved tier-1 tickets (shipping, returns, sizing) and dynamically escalated complex queries to human staff.
