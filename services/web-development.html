FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
A basic business website typically takes 1–3 weeks. Larger projects with custom features can take 4–10 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before we begin.
Can I update the website myself after it's built?
Yes! We build on platforms like WordPress and Wix that let you edit content easily. We also provide training so your team can manage it confidently.
Do you handle hosting and domain registration?
Yes, we can manage hosting, domain registration, and SSL certificates for you. We recommend reliable solutions based on your traffic and budget.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive - it works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. We test across all major devices.
