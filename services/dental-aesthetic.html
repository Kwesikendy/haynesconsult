Dental Care

Dental Services

Comprehensive oral health care - from routine check-ups to advanced restorative treatments, all delivered with the highest standard of clinical care.

Dental Consultation

Regular check-ups and oral health screenings to detect early signs of dental problems like cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer. Prevention and early treatment are at the core of everything we do.

Dental Fillings

Used to restore teeth damaged by decay. The decayed portion of the tooth is carefully removed and replaced with a filling material - composite, amalgam, or ceramic - to restore shape, function, and strength.

Root Canal Treatment

When infection reaches the inner pulp of a tooth, root canal treatment saves it from extraction. Using modern techniques and effective anaesthesia, we make the procedure as comfortable as possible.

Tooth Replacement

We restore missing or damaged teeth with natural-looking solutions - crowns (to cover and protect a tooth), bridges (to replace one or more missing teeth), and dentures (removable replacements for multiple missing teeth). Helping you chew better and smile confidently.

Dental Jewels

Add a touch of sparkle to your smile with safe, non-invasive tooth gems. Applied without drilling or damage, they enhance your personal style and can be removed at any time with no residue.

Teeth Whitening

A safe, professional cosmetic procedure that lightens teeth and removes stains or discoloration - giving you a noticeably brighter, more attractive smile. Faster and more effective than over-the-counter solutions.

Extractions

When a tooth is severely decayed, damaged, or causing crowding, it may need to be removed. Our extractions are performed with utmost care and effective pain control to ensure your comfort throughout.

Aesthetic Consultations

Get expert advice tailored to your facial and dental aesthetics. We listen to your goals and create a personalised treatment plan to help you look and feel your absolute best - naturally and confidently.

Dental Tattoos

Express your personality with unique, safe dental art applied to your teeth. A standout, creative way to personalise your smile with intricate designs - fully reversible and applied without damaging enamel.

Aesthetic Medicine

Aesthetic Medicine Services

Non-surgical aesthetic treatments to refresh and restore your natural look - administered by experienced clinical professionals.

Dermal Fillers

Smooth out deep lines, restore lost facial volume, and contour features with hyaluronic acid fillers. Results are immediate and natural-looking - no surgery, no downtime. Ideal for cheeks, jawline, and nasolabial folds.

Lip Fillers

Enhance lip shape, add volume, and define the lip border for fuller, well-defined lips that naturally complement your facial features. We focus on balanced, beautiful results - never overdone.

Botox

Relax facial expression lines and prevent new wrinkles with precise, professional Botox treatments. Smooth forehead lines, crow's feet, and frown lines safely - with results lasting 3–6 months.
