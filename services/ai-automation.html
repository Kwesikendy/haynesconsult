FAQ

Common Questions

Will the AI chatbot understand my customers' specific questions?
Yes. We train the chatbot on your business data, products, services, and FAQs. The more you share with us, the smarter it gets. We also continuously improve it based on real conversations.
Do my team need to be technical to use the automation?
Not at all. We build dashboards and interfaces that your team can use without any technical knowledge. We also provide training sessions and documentation.
How long does it take to deploy an AI solution?
Simple chatbots can be deployed in 1–2 weeks. More complex automation systems typically take 3–8 weeks depending on integrations required.
Is my data safe?
Security is our priority. All systems are built with encryption, role-based access, and industry-standard data protection. For healthcare clients, we comply with HIPAA requirements.
