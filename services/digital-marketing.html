FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to design a logo or brand identity?
A logo with concept options typically takes 5–7 business days. A full brand identity package (logo, colours, typography, brand guide) takes 2–3 weeks. We present multiple options at each stage for your feedback.
Can I see examples of your work?
Absolutely. We can share client case studies and portfolio samples during your consultation. Just reach out and we'll put together a relevant showcase for your industry.
Do you manage social media on an ongoing basis?
Yes! We offer monthly retainer packages for full social media management - including content creation, design, scheduling, and community engagement. Contact us for current packages.
How soon will I see results from digital marketing?
Paid ads can show results within days. SEO typically takes 3–6 months to build momentum. We set clear expectations and track measurable KPIs from day one.
